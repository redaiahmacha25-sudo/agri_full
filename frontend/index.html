🔄 How the Procurement Workflow Works

1
Farmer Registers & Submits — Log in and submit a sell request with crop type, quantity, and image proof.
2
Sachivalayam Verification — Local employee visits/reviews the request and marks it as Verified or Rejected with reason.
3
Admin Approval & Scheduling — District admin approves and schedules the procurement date and location.
4
Procurement & Payment — Government team arrives on scheduled date. Payment transferred directly to farmer's bank account.
