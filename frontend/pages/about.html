Why AgriConnect Exists
Problems We Are Solving

Indian farmers have long faced systemic exploitation. AgriConnect directly addresses these critical issues with technology and governance.

🕵️

Middlemen Exploitation

Farmers were forced to sell crops through agents and brokers who would purchase below MSP and resell at market rate, keeping profits that rightfully belonged to farmers.

✅ Solution: Direct government procurement at MSP
📋

Manual & Opaque Processes

Paper-based procurement was slow, error-prone, and opaque. Farmers had no visibility into the status of their requests, payments, or grievances.

✅ Solution: Full digital workflow with real-time tracking
⏳

Delayed Payments

Farmers waited months for payment after crop procurement. Multiple intermediaries in the payment chain caused delays and sometimes loss of funds entirely.

✅ Solution: Direct bank transfer upon procurement completion
🗣️

No Grievance Redressal

Farmers had no formal mechanism to report exploitation, subsidy issues, or crop damage. Complaints were often dismissed or ignored at local level.

✅ Solution: Structured grievance system with escalation
💸

MSP Non-Compliance

Despite government mandated MSP, enforcement was poor. Without a digital trail, proving below-MSP transactions was nearly impossible for farmers.

✅ Solution: All transactions recorded at government-set MSP
🌐

Information Asymmetry

Farmers lacked access to real-time crop prices, government schemes, and support information — keeping them dependent on local agents for even basic information.

✅ Solution: Open access MSP dashboard and scheme information
Our Purpose
Mission & Vision
🎯

Our Mission

To create a transparent, technology-driven agricultural procurement ecosystem that ensures every farmer in Andhra Pradesh receives fair, timely compensation for their produce — directly from the government, without any intermediary.

  • Eliminate exploitative middlemen from the supply chain
  • Ensure 100% MSP compliance across all procurement
  • Digitize every step of farmer-government interaction
  • Empower farmers with information and agency
  • Build accountability into governance processes
🔭

Our Vision

A future where every farmer in Andhra Pradesh — regardless of literacy, location, or landholding — has equal, dignified access to government procurement, support, and information through a seamless digital platform.

  • Andhra Pradesh as a model for digital agricultural governance
  • Complete elimination of cash-based procurement
  • Real-time visibility for every farmer transaction
  • Integrated welfare delivery through single platform
  • Scalable to all 26 districts and 13 million farmers
Platform Capabilities
Key Features of AgriConnect

A comprehensive suite of tools designed for farmers, field employees, and district administrators.

📤

Direct Crop Procurement

Farmers submit sell requests digitally with crop details, quantity, and photo proof. Requests are processed through a verified 3-tier approval workflow.

🔍

Real-Time Status Tracking

Every stage — submission, verification, approval, scheduling, and payment — is visible to the farmer in real time with timestamp logs.

📩

Grievance Management

Farmers can raise complaints, subsidy requests, and crop damage reports. Each case is tracked through Sachivalayam to Admin with full escalation support.

💰

Direct Bank Payments

Once procurement is complete, payment is credited directly to the farmer's registered bank account with a transaction reference for full traceability.

👥

Role-Based Governance

Three-tier access control — Farmer, Sachivalayam Employee, District Admin — ensures proper accountability and separation of duties in all workflows.

📊

Analytics & Reporting

District administrators get real-time dashboards with procurement analytics, payment summaries, grievance resolution rates, and crop-wise data.

How It Works
The Governance Workflow

A structured three-tier process ensures accountability, transparency, and efficiency at every step.

👨‍🌾
1

Farmer

Initiates all requests and tracks outcomes

  • Register on portal
  • Submit sell request
  • Upload crop image
  • Track request status
  • Raise grievances
  • Receive payment
🧑‍💼
2

Sachivalayam Employee

Field-level verification and first-line support

  • Receive assigned requests
  • Field visit & verification
  • Verify or reject with reason
  • Handle service requests
  • Escalate to Admin
🏛️
3

District Admin

Final authority on all decisions and payments

  • Review verified requests
  • Approve & schedule
  • Manage crop MSP prices
  • Process payments
  • Handle escalations
  • System analytics
